# Privacy Policy for Looper

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Overview

Looper is a browser extension that allows users to control video playback speed on any website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

Looper collects and stores **only** the following data:

- **Playback speed preferences**: Your preferred playback speed for each website you visit (e.g., 1.5x for youtube.com)

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- Browsing history
- Video content or URLs
- Analytics or usage data
- Any data that could identify you

## Data Storage

All data is stored **locally on your device** using Chrome's built-in storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). Your preferences:

- Never leave your device
- Are not transmitted to any external servers
- Are not shared with any third parties

## Data Retention

Your speed preferences are stored indefinitely on your device until:

- You uninstall the extension
- You clear your browser data
- You manually reset the preferences

## Permissions

Looper requires the following permission:

- **Storage**: To save your playback speed preferences locally

## Third-Party Services

Looper does **not** use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
